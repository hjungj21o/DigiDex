Welcome to DigiDex!


DigiDex is an online business card that you can edit and interact with.

The directions are simple:
1. Simply hover over the card to see the other side of the card.
2. Customize your card by clicking on the "Edit" button on the left hand side.
3. Play around with it! The world is your oyster.


We hope to bring you more functionality in the future, including:
1. Iframe embedding feature so that you can actually utilize it on your website.
2. Save/print as PDF so that you can bring it to a print shop.
3. Additional Templates.
4. And so much more!

To close, click anywhere outside the box or the "x" button.
